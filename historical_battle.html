Famous Historical Battles

  • Battle of Thermopylae

    Date: 480 BCE

    Description: The Battle of Thermopylae was fought between an alliance of Greek city-states, led by King Leonidas I of Sparta, and the Persian Empire, led by King Xerxes I. The outnumbered Greeks held off the Persian forces for several days before ultimately being defeated.

  • Battle of Waterloo

    Date: June 18, 1815

    Description: The Battle of Waterloo was a significant battle during the Napoleonic Wars. It took place near the town of Waterloo in present-day Belgium and resulted in the defeat of Napoleon Bonaparte by the British-led Allied forces, marking the end of his reign as Emperor of the French.

  • Battle of Gettysburg

    Date: July 1-3, 1863

    Description: The Battle of Gettysburg was a major engagement of the American Civil War. It was fought between the Confederate Army of Northern Virginia, commanded by General Robert E. Lee, and the Union Army of the Potomac, led by General George G. Meade. The battle resulted in a Union victory and is considered a turning point in the war.

  • Battle of Hastings

    Date: October 14, 1066

    Description: The Battle of Hastings was a decisive Norman victory in the Norman Conquest of England. It was fought between the Norman-French army of William the Conqueror and the English army led by King Harold II. The battle led to the Norman conquest of England and had a significant impact on English history.

  • Battle of Stalingrad

    Date: August 23, 1942 - February 2, 1943

    Description: The Battle of Stalingrad was a major confrontation of World War II, fought between the Soviet Union and Nazi Germany. It was one of the bloodiest battles in history and marked a turning point in the war. The Soviet victory at Stalingrad halted the German advance into the Soviet Union and marked the beginning of the end for the Axis powers in Eastern Europe.
